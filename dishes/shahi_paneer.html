shahi paneer

Description:

Shahi Paneer is a royal Mughlai curry made with soft paneer cubes simmered in a creamy, rich gravy of tomatoes, onions, nuts, and spices. Mildly sweet and fragrant, it’s a luxurious vegetarian delight served with naan or rice.

Ingredients:

Paneer, onions, tomato purée, ginger-garlic paste, cashews, fresh cream, milk, ghee or oil, cumin seeds, turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, salt, saffron strands (optional), fresh coriander or cream for garnish.

Steps:

  1. In a pan, heat ghee or oil. Sauté chopped onions, garlic, ginger, green chilies, and cashews until soft. Let it cool, then blend into a smooth paste.
  2. In the same pan, add a little more ghee. Pour in the paste and cook until it thickens. Add tomato purée and cook until oil separates.
  3. Mix in turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt. Stir well and cook for a few minutes to release flavors.
  4. Lower the heat and add fresh cream and a little milk. Stir continuously to get a rich, smooth, royal gravy.
  5. Gently add paneer cubes. Simmer for 5–7 minutes so the paneer absorbs the flavors without breaking.
  6. Garnish with chopped coriander or a swirl of cream. Serve hot with naan, roti, or jeera rice. A rich, royal North Indian delight!
