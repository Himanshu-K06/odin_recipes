Chicken Korma

  1. In a bowl, mix chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes to enhance flavor.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a pan. Saut√© sliced onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnish and blend the rest into a paste with soaked cashews or almonds.
  3. In the same pan, add whole spices (cardamom, cloves, bay leaf), then the onion-nut paste. Cook well, then add coriander powder, garam masala, and a little water.
  4. Add marinated chicken to the masala. Cook on medium heat until the chicken is browned and sealed with the spice mix.
  5. Add more yogurt and a bit of cream or milk. Cover and simmer on low heat until the chicken is tender and the gravy turns rich and aromatic.
  6. Top with fried onions and chopped coriander. Serve hot with naan, paratha, or rice for a rich, Mughlai-style meal.
