CHOLE BHATURE

  1. Soak 1 cup of chickpeas overnight. In the morning, pressure cook them with salt and enough water until they are soft and tender.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and sauté finely chopped onions until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste, then chopped tomatoes. Cook until soft.
  3. Mix in turmeric, cumin, coriander powder, red chili powder, and garam masala. Stir well, then add the cooked chickpeas. Simmer until thickened and flavorful.
  4. In a bowl, combine 2 cups all-purpose flour, salt, baking soda, and ½ cup yogurt. Add water as needed to form a soft dough. Rest for 2 hours.
  5. Divide the dough, roll into discs, and deep-fry in hot oil until they puff and turn golden brown.
  6. Plate hot bhature with spicy chole. Garnish with sliced onions, lemon wedges, and optionally green chilies. Enjoy as a hearty meal.
